## Output Guidance

Keep the blueprint concrete enough for implementation.

If information is missing, do not invent it. Use:

- `要確認：`
- `仮置き：`
- `推奨：`

The first-view copy should be short enough for mobile.

The site structure may be adjusted by project type. For example, an LP may not need many lower-page assumptions, while a store site needs access and opening hours.
